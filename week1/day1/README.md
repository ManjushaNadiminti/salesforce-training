# Day 1 - Salesforce CRM Basics

## What is Salesforce?
Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used to manage customers, sales, and business workflows.

## What is CRM?
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management. It helps businesses manage customer data and interactions.

## Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

- Lead = Potential customer
- Contact = Person associated with company
- Opportunity = Possible sales deal
- Customer = Final buyer

## Account, Contact, Opportunity

### Account
Represents a company or organization.

### Contact
Represents a person related to the company.

### Opportunity
Represents a business deal or sales process.

## Real World Mapping - College Admission

- Account = College
- Contact = Student
- Lead = Student inquiry
- Opportunity = Admission process
